== Help ==

WikiMindMap is a simple mindmap to navigate trough Wiki content.

The mindmap has the main topic in the centre, topic groups [ ] on the first level and and related topics [ ] on the outmost level. Topic groups can be opened by a click on the group box.

A click on the following symbols help you to browse the mindmap:
   
Text Clicking on the text will bring you to the corresponding position in the Wiki.
This symbol brings the related topic to the center and makes it the main topic.
This brings you to a non Wiki web page
